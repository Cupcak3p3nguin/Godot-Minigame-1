# Godot Minigame 1

## Devlog

Nodes are like ingredients, and scenes are the dishes. This is because nodes are what make up scenes. Depending on what nodes you add, the outcome of the scenes will vary. In cooking, different ingredients can make your dish turn out a certain way. If you add many spicy ingredients, your dish will turn out spicy. Same as how, depending on what nodes you add to a scene, it can change whether the scene is for a character, level, or something else.
